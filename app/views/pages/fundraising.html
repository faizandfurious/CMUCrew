Would You Like To Rent A Rower?

Our rowers would love to help you move, do housework, garden or really anything you can think of for the low charge of $14 per hour per rower. To arrange please contact our fundraising chairs.

Recent News

Carnegie Mellon Crew has recently partnered with Gwen's Girls, a Pittsburgh outreach group for underpriviledged young girls, to provide homework help and STEM education through student led activities.

Additional fundraising opportunities include working at Pittsburgh Steelers games as well as Carnegie Mellon football and soccer games.

Our current fundraising chairs are Kelsey Diffley (fundraising@tartancrew.org) and Jinie Haytko (fundraising@tartancrew.org).
